# Hosted revenue path

The public repository owns the local truth layer: CLI, adapter contract, normalizer, materiality policy, public corpus, and GitHub workflow.

A paid service becomes credible only where open-source local execution cannot remove recurring operational cost:

- managed Excel Web and Google Sheets execution;
- private fixtures and organization-scoped baselines;
- zero-retention and regional processing policy;
- pull-request status checks and retained evidence;
- scheduled engine/version regression runs;
- team materiality policies, audit logs, SSO, and support;
- user-owned Windows/desktop runners with policy control;
- larger files, concurrency, and longer history.

This is a revenue hypothesis, not validated demand. Buyer outreach remains separately approval-gated. The commercial dossier requires 20 qualified conversations, five pilot accepts, and two statements of willingness to pay at least $199/month before the company thesis passes.
